# FlashTrendy User Guide

Welcome to FlashTrendy! This guide will help you navigate our store and manage your account.

## Browsing Products

- **Search**: Use the search bar in the header to find specific products.
- **Categories**: Browse products by category using the navigation menu.
- **Filters**: On the product listing page, use the sidebar to filter by price, rating, and category.
- **Sorting**: Sort products by price, newest arrivals, or customer ratings.

## Making a Purchase

1. **Add to Cart**: Click "Add to Cart" on any product page or quick view.
2. **Review Cart**: Click the cart icon to see your items, update quantities, or remove items.
3. **Checkout**: Click "Proceed to Checkout". You will be asked to sign in if you haven't already.
4. **Shipping**: Provide or select a shipping address.
5. **Payment**: Securely complete your purchase using Stripe.
6. **Confirmation**: You will receive an order confirmation on-screen and via email.

## Managing Your Account

Access your dashboard by clicking on your profile icon in the header.

- **Orders**: View your past and current orders, track status, and view details.
- **Profile**: Update your name, email, and profile picture.
- **Security**: Change your password or manage linked authentication accounts.
- **Addresses**: Save multiple shipping addresses for faster checkout.
- **Wishlist**: Save products you're interested in for later.

## Notifications

Check the notification bell for updates on your orders, special offers, and account activity.
